Articles

At Back to the Light, we are dedicated to promoting the mental health and emotional well-being of abused and vulnerable children in rural areas
through the transformative power of art therapy. Our website serves as a gateway to our organization, providing valuable resources, information,
and opportunities for engagement. Here, parents, guardians, donors, volunteers, and other stakeholders can learn about our initiatives, programs,
and the positive impact of art therapy on the lives of vulnerable children.
