ABOUT US

At Back to the Light, we are dedicated to promoting the mental health and emotional well-being of abused and vulnerable children in rural areas
through the transformative power of art therapy. Our website serves as a gateway to our organization, providing valuable resources, information,
and opportunities for engagement. Here, parents, guardians, donors, volunteers, and other stakeholders can learn about our initiatives, programs,
and the positive impact of art therapy on the lives of vulnerable children.

OUR STORY

Back to the Light was born from a deep passion to make
a meaningful impact in the lives of abused and vulnerable
children in rural areas. It began with a shared vision among
a group of dedicated people who are recognized the healing
power of art therapy and its potential to create positive
change.

OUR TEAM

our team is a cohesive and compassionate group of people
who are deeply committed to making a difference in the
lives of abused and vulnerable children. Each member brings
a unique set of skills, experiences, and a shared passion for
empowering young hearts through art therapy.
