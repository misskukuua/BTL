BACK TO THE LIGHT

We empower abused and vulnerable children in rural areas by providing access to art therapy,
fostering emotional healing, and promoting their overall well-being. Through creative expression,
we strive to create a safe and nurturing environment where children can discover their inner
strength, develop coping strategies, and regain their joy and self-worth. Our website serves as
a gateway to our organization, providing valuable resources, information, and opportunities for
engagement. Here, parents, guardians, donors, volunteers, and other stakeholders can learn
about our initiatives, programs, and the positive impact of art therapy on the lives of vulnerable
children.

PROGRAMES & SERVICES

CHILD SUPPORT

The Child Support program aims to address emotional scars, build coping strategies, and cultivate self-esteem.
We strive to break the barriers of fear and silence, encouraging children to share their feelings, hopes, and dreams
through various art forms. Through this transformative process, children not only find healing but also develop a stronger sense of self and renewed hope for the future.

CHILD COUNSELING

Child counseling is an essential aspect of our services at Back to the Light, where we are committed to supporting
abused and vulnerable children in their journey to healing and growth. Our team of trained counselors and therapists
create a safe space for children to express their feelings, fears, and concerns. We engage children in the healing process, empowering them to process their emotions and develop healthy coping strategies.

GUIDNACE COUNSELING

Our guidance counseling sessions are rooted in empathy, understanding, and non-judgment. We create a safe and confidential space where you can freely express your thoughts, emotions, and aspirations. Our counselors are trained to actively listen, assess your unique situation, and collaboratively work with you to identify your strengths and areas for growth.
